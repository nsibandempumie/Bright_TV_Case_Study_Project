# 📖 Case Study Overview

Bright TV aims to increase its subscriber base by gaining a deeper understanding of viewer behaviour and content consumption.

This case study analyses Bright TV's viewership dataset to identify viewing trends, evaluate TV channel performance, understand audience demographics, and generate actionable business recommendations that support strategic decision-making.

The project follows a complete data analytics workflow, beginning with business understanding and concluding with executive reporting and dashboard development.

---

# 🎯 Business Objective

The objective of this project was to:

- Analyse customer viewing behaviour.
- Identify content consumption patterns.
- Evaluate TV channel performance.
- Understand audience demographics.
- Develop interactive dashboards for business users.
- Present actionable recommendations that support subscriber growth.

---

# ❓ Business Questions

This project was designed to answer the following questions:

- Which TV channels receive the highest number of views?
- Which channels generate the highest watch time?
- What times of day experience peak viewership?
- Which days of the week receive the highest engagement?
- Which provinces contribute the most viewers?
- Which age groups are most active?
- How does viewership differ by gender and race?
- What business opportunities can improve customer engagement?

Before analysis, the dataset was cleaned and transformed to improve data quality.
